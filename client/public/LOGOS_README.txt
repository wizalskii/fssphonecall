LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Place the following logo files in this directory:

1. vatsim-logo.png - VATSIM official logo
   - Recommended size: 200x200 pixels or similar aspect ratio
   - Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
   - Download from: https://www.vatsim.net/ (official VATSIM resources)

2. zlc-logo.png - ZLC ARTCC (Salt Lake City ARTCC) logo
   - Recommended size: 200x200 pixels or similar aspect ratio
   - Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
   - Obtain from ZLC ARTCC official sources

The logos will be displayed on the home page at a height of 64px (h-16).

If logos are not present, the application will gracefully fall back to text-only display.
