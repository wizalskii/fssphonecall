A: Integrated LCD List

Station list appears directly on the radio's LCD. Arrow keys/buttons to scroll, selected station highlights in green. The main display shows the selected frequency. Feels like tuning a real radio — everything stays inside the device.

B: Separate Directory Panel

Radio stays compact at top showing active connection. A separate "Station Directory" panel below lists all online controllers with LED status indicators (green=available, amber=busy). Click a station to tune. Two distinct hardware units stacked.

C: Dual-Display (Active/Standby)

Mimics real aircraft COM radios with Active and Standby displays side by side. Arrow buttons scroll through available stations on the standby side, XFER button swaps standby to active and initiates the call. Most realistic to actual aviation radio operation.
